Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Important: By using our mobile application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account and use our app, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account creation, authentication, and important notifications
  • Display name - Optional, used for personalizing your experience
  • User ID - Automatically generated unique identifier

1.2 Search and Usage Data

  • Search queries - Keywords, price ranges, locations you search for
  • Search schedules - Frequency and timing of your automated searches
  • Saved listings - Items you mark as favorites or view
  • Notification preferences - Your choices for receiving alerts

1.3 Device Information

  • Device tokens - For sending push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Device type and OS version - For compatibility and troubleshooting
  • App version - To ensure you're using the latest features

1.4 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage statistics - How you interact with the app (anonymized)
  • Crash reports - To fix bugs and improve stability
  • Ad interaction data - If you interact with advertisements (collected by AdMob)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Provide the service - Execute your searches and deliver notifications
  • Authenticate your account - Secure login via Azure AD B2C
  • Store your preferences - Remember your search settings and schedules
  • Send notifications - Alert you when new listings match your searches

2.2 Improvements and Analytics

  • Improve performance - Analyze usage patterns to optimize the app
  • Fix bugs - Diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Develop features - Understand what features users want

2.3 Communication

  • Service updates - Important announcements about the app
  • Support - Respond to your questions and issues
  • Marketing - Optional promotional messages (you can opt out)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Primary storage - Microsoft Azure servers (Europe region)
  • Database - Azure PostgreSQL with encryption at rest
  • Backups - Encrypted and stored in Azure secure storage

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption - All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication - Industry-standard OAuth 2.0 via Azure AD B2C
  • Access controls - Strict role-based access to your data
  • Regular updates - We keep security patches current

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data - Retained while your account is active
  • Search history - Retained for up to 90 days or until deleted by you
  • Notification logs - Retained for 30 days for troubleshooting
  • Analytics data - Anonymized and retained indefinitely

After account deletion, we remove all personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct your data - Update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your data - Request complete account deletion
  • Export your data - Download your searches and settings
  • Opt out of marketing - Unsubscribe from promotional emails

6.2 Notification Controls

  • Manage notification preferences in the app settings
  • Disable notifications in your device settings

6.3 Ad Personalization

  • Opt out of personalized ads in Android settings (Google Ads Settings)
  • Upgrade to Premium to remove all ads

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To access, correct, or delete your data, you can:

  • Use the account settings within the app
  • Contact us at the email address below

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be informed - This privacy policy
  • Right of access - Request your personal data
  • Right to rectification - Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure - Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing - Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability - Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object - Object to certain types of processing

Legal basis for processing:

  • Contract - Processing necessary to provide the service
  • Consent - You explicitly agreed (e.g., marketing emails)
  • Legitimate interests - Improving and securing our service

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
